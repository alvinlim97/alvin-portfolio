Digital OnBoarding
Description
Apr 2020 – Present

Digital OnBoarding is a web application for Hong Leong Bank Malaysia to provide a ‘Know Your Customer’ (KYC) detail submission before processing application on opening business account of different business type such as Sole Proprietor or SDN BHD. Customer able to register to this submission by inputting their business registration number on different location such as Peninsular Malaysia or Sabah & Sarawak.

Customer will required to fill up some personal & business detail to ease and shorten time for future processing any application on Hong Leong Bank Malaysia services. Customer can make appointment on all Hong Leong Bank branches from Malaysia. System will provide save function on the incomplete form for customer to continue next time by entering their registered business registration number and verify TAC from their mobile phone. Final submitted application will send to selected branch for future reference and process on the appointment and application.
